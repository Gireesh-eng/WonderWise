🌴 10-Day Luxury Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Seminyak

Morning

Arrive at Ngurah Rai International Airport, private transfer to luxury villa in Seminyak.

Afternoon

Check-in, relax by private pool, explore Seminyak Beach.

Evening

Welcome dinner at Ku De Ta beachfront restaurant with sunset views.

🏖️ Luxury Tip: Private beach butler service available at your villa!

Day 2: Surf Lessons & Spa

Morning

Professional surf lessons at Batu Bolong Beach, beginner-friendly waves.

Afternoon

Traditional Balinese spa treatment at luxury spa resort.

Evening

Dinner at Sarong Restaurant, contemporary Indonesian cuisine.

🏄‍♂️ Surf Tip: Best waves are typically 6-8 AM and 4-6 PM!

Day 3: Cultural Ubud

Morning

Private driver to Ubud, visit Tegallalang Rice Terraces.

Afternoon

Explore Sacred Monkey Forest, traditional art markets in Ubud center.

Evening

Traditional Kecak fire dance performance, dinner at Mozaic Restaurant.

🎭 Cultural Tip: Dress modestly when visiting temples!

Day 4: Volcano Adventure

Early Morning

Mount Batur sunrise trek (4:30 AM start), breakfast at summit.

Afternoon

Relax at Tirta Empul Holy Spring Temple, natural hot springs.

Evening

Return to Seminyak, casual dinner at beach warung.

⛰️ Trek Tip: Bring warm layers - it's cold at 4 AM!

Day 5: Beach Club & Relaxation

Morning

Leisurely breakfast, private beach time with water sports.

Afternoon

Day pass at Potato Head Beach Club, infinity pool and beach access.

Evening

Sunset cocktails, seafood dinner at Jimbaran Bay.

🍹 Luxury Tip: Try the signature cocktails at sunset!

Day 6: Nusa Penida Day Trip

Morning

Fast boat to Nusa Penida island, visit Kelingking Beach.

Afternoon

Angel's Billabong and Broken Beach, snorkeling at Crystal Bay.

Evening

Return to Bali, relaxing dinner at villa.

🏊‍♂️ Snorkel Tip: Bring underwater camera for amazing photos!

Day 7: Surfing & Canggu

Morning

Advanced surf session at Uluwatu or Padang Padang.

Afternoon

Explore Canggu area, visit trendy cafés and beach clubs.

Evening

Uluwatu Temple for sunset, traditional Kecak dance.

Day 8: Wellness & Yoga

Morning

Private yoga session at villa overlooking rice fields.

Afternoon

Full day spa package: massage, facial, body scrub.

Evening

Healthy dinner at organic restaurant, meditation session.

Day 9: Adventure & Shopping

Morning

White water rafting on Ayung River, jungle adventure.

Afternoon

Shopping at Seminyak boutiques, local handicrafts.

Evening

Farewell dinner at fine dining restaurant, beach walk.

Day 10: Departure

Morning

Final breakfast at villa, last-minute shopping.

Afternoon

Private transfer to airport, departure.

✈️ Travel Tip: Allow 3 hours for international flight check-in!

🏛️ Key Attractions

Seminyak Beach

Premium beach with luxury resorts, beach clubs, and excellent surf conditions.

Best Time: Sunset for golden hour photos

Activities: Surfing, beach clubs, shopping

Uluwatu Beach

Dramatic clifftop setting with consistent waves, perfect for experienced surfers.

Best Time: Early morning or late afternoon

Activities: Surfing, temple visits, Kecak dance

Uluwatu Temple

Clifftop temple with spectacular ocean views and traditional Kecak dance performances.

Best Time: Sunset

Admission: IDR 30,000

Tirta Empul Temple

Holy spring temple where locals and visitors participate in purification rituals.

Best Time: Early morning

Admission: IDR 50,000

Mount Batur Sunrise Trek

Active volcano trek with breathtaking sunrise views and breakfast at the summit.

Best Time: Start at 4:30 AM

Cost: IDR 500,000 with guide

Ayung River Rafting

Class II-III rapids through lush jungle scenery and traditional villages.

Best Time: Morning

Cost: IDR 400,000 per person

🤝 Cultural Etiquette

Temple Etiquette

  • Dress modestly, cover shoulders and knees
  • Remove shoes before entering
  • Don't point feet toward altars
  • Be respectful during ceremonies

General Behavior

  • Use right hand for giving/receiving
  • Avoid public displays of affection
  • Don't touch someone's head
  • Smile and be polite

Beach Etiquette

  • Respect local customs at religious sites
  • Don't litter on beaches
  • Be mindful of surfing etiquette
  • Support local businesses
