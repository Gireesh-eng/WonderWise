📅 7-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Asakusa

Morning

Arrive at Narita Airport, take Skyliner to Ueno. Check into capsule hotel in Asakusa.

Afternoon

Visit Senso-ji Temple, explore Nakamise Shopping Street. Traditional lunch at local restaurant.

Evening

Dinner in Asakusa, early rest to recover from jet lag.

💡 Tip: Get a 7-day JR Pass for unlimited train travel!

Day 2: Modern Tokyo

Morning

Visit Tokyo Skytree, explore Sumida Aquarium. Coffee at local café.

Afternoon

Shibuya Crossing, Hachiko Statue, shopping in Shibuya Center Gai.

Evening

Robot Restaurant in Shinjuku, explore Golden Gai bars.

💡 Tip: Download Google Translate app with camera feature!

Day 3: Traditional Culture

Morning

Visit Meiji Shrine, walk through Yoyogi Park.

Afternoon

Explore Harajuku, visit Omotesando Hills. Traditional tea ceremony experience.

Evening

Dinner in Shinjuku, visit observation deck for night views.

Day 4: Tsukiji & Ginza

Morning

Early morning at Tsukiji Outer Market, fresh sushi breakfast.

Afternoon

Explore Ginza district, visit department stores and galleries.

Evening

Upscale dining in Ginza, explore night markets.

Day 5: Imperial Palace & Akihabara

Morning

Imperial Palace East Gardens, learn about Japanese history.

Afternoon

Electronics and anime culture in Akihabara, maid café experience.

Evening

Dinner in Akihabara, explore game centers and electronics shops.

Day 6: Ueno & Museums

Morning

Ueno Park, visit Tokyo National Museum.

Afternoon

Ueno Zoo, Ameyoko Market for souvenir shopping.

Evening

Farewell dinner at traditional izakaya.

Day 7: Departure

Morning

Last-minute shopping in Tokyo Station, departure to airport.

💡 Tip: Allow 2-3 hours for airport procedures!

🏛️ Key Attractions

Senso-ji Temple

Tokyo's oldest temple, founded in 628 AD. Experience traditional Japanese architecture and spiritual atmosphere.

Best Time: Early morning or late afternoon

Admission: Free

Meiji Shrine

Dedicated to Emperor Meiji, surrounded by a peaceful forest in the heart of Tokyo.

Best Time: Early morning

Admission: Free

Tokyo Skytree

World's second tallest structure, offering spectacular views of Tokyo.

Best Time: Sunset for day and night views

Admission: ¥2,060 for main deck

Shibuya Crossing

World's busiest pedestrian crossing, iconic symbol of modern Tokyo.

Best Time: Rush hours for full experience

Admission: Free

Robot Restaurant

Colorful robot show with music, dancing, and entertainment.

Best Time: Evening shows

Admission: ¥8,000

Tsukiji Outer Market

Fresh seafood and traditional Japanese street food.

Best Time: Early morning

Admission: Free (food costs extra)

🗣️ Essential Japanese Phrases

Basic Greetings

Hello: Konnichiwa (kon-nee-chee-wah)

Thank you: Arigato gozaimasu (ah-ree-gah-toh goh-zah-ee-mahs)

Excuse me: Sumimasen (soo-mee-mah-sen)

Dining

Menu please: Menyū wo kudasai (men-yoo oh koo-dah-sah-ee)

Delicious: Oishii (oh-ee-shee)

Bill please: Okaikei wo kudasai (oh-kah-ee-kay oh koo-dah-sah-ee)

🤝 Cultural Etiquette

Temple Etiquette

  • Bow before entering
  • Remove hat and sunglasses
  • Speak quietly
  • Follow photography rules

Dining Rules

  • Say "Itadakimasu" before eating
  • Don't stick chopsticks upright in rice
  • Slurping noodles is acceptable
  • Don't pass food chopstick to chopstick
